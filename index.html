Welcome to Elev8!

Elev8 is a community where students can explore science, technology, engineering, and math in ways that are creative, hands-on, and open to everyone. Our mission is to elevate the next generation of thinkers, creators, and changemakers.

Our Approach

  • Hands-on STEM projects guided by high school mentors
  • Creative problem-solving with coding and electronics
  • Encouraging curiosity, growth, and collaboration
  • Building confidence through exploration and achievement

Get Involved

Whether you’re a student, parent, or volunteer, there’s a place for you at Elev8. Explore our projects, join our workshops, or connect with our community to start lifting each other up!
