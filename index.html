Welcome to Elev8

Elev8 is a student-led organization dedicated to inspiring curiosity and creativity through STEAM. Our goal is to give every student the opportunity to explore, create, and learn without limits.

Through fun coding workshops, hands-on STEM projects, and collaborative learning experiences, we help students discover their potential and elevate their skills to infinity.

Programs and Projects

We offer a range of programs where students can explore Scratch programming, basic HTML, and STEM concepts. Each project introduces new skills and encourages creative problem-solving.

  • Interactive Coding Lessons (Grades 1–4)
  • STEM Exploration Projects
  • Creative Animations & Games

How We Teach

Our high school volunteers guide elementary students through hands-on lessons. Students learn coding fundamentals, teamwork, and logical thinking while having fun creating games, stories, and animations.
