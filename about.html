About Elev8

Elev8 is a student-led organization dedicated to empowering students through STEAM education. We push boundaries and create opportunities to learn and innovate.

Our Vision

We imagine a space where students can explore science, tech, engineering, and math in ways that are creative, hands-on, and open to everyone. Elev8 is about helping each other grow, try new things, and see that there’s no limit to what we can build or become.
